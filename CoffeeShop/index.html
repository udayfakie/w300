Our Story

The Cozy Bean started with a simple dream: to create a welcoming space where people could enjoy exceptional coffee and connect with their community. Founded in 1993, we've been committed to sourcing the finest beans and crafting each cup with care.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a neighborhood favorite, known for our friendly baristas, cozy atmosphere, and passion for all things coffee. We believe in the power of a good cup of coffee to brighten your day and bring people together.
